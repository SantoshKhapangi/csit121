Chicken Dumplings

"the best dumplings I have ever made"

Ingredients

  • boneless chicken
  • dumplings wrap
  • ginger garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • chopped onions and cabbage
  • Turmeric powder
  • Corriander
  • red chillies
  • cumin powder

Instructions

  1. Bring a large bowl and add boneless chicken, chopped onions, cabbage and corriander.
  2. Add spices like red chillies, cumin powder, salt to taste and ginger garlic paste.
  3. Mix well and keep it marinated for half hour.
  4. Heat one table spoon oil in a pan over medium flame and add a small portion of turmeric powder on it.
  5. Add that heated oil with turmeric powder on the marinated chicken with vegeatables and spices.
  6. Put one table spoon filling in the center of dumplings wrap and lift the edge one side and start pleating. Join them in the center to seal it.
  7. Heat 1-2 glass of water in a steamer or a deep vessel over medium flame.
  8. Place stand in the steamer and place dumplings over it.Cover the steamer/vessel with a lid and steam them for 15 to 20 minutes.
  9. Finally tranfer the dumplings in a plate and serve with schezwan sauce or dumplings chutney.

This is my favorite recipe because it reminds me of my family who are far away from me. We used to make it together when I was back in my country with my family.
